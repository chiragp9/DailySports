Aiming to beat last year's total of $18,429,613, Valve is presenting Dota 2 players with the International 2016 Battle Pass.

FORMAT

- Open Qualifiers - June 21st - June 24th, 2016
Eight Regional Single-elimination Open Qualifiers, two brackets for each region.
The winners of each Regional Qualifier will advance to the respective Main Qualifier.
- Main Qualifiers - June 25th - June 28th, 2016
Four Regional Qualifiers.
One group, with the winner advancing directly to The International.
Second through fifth place teams advance to the Regional Double-elimination Playoff Bracket.
The winner of each Regional Playoff Bracket will join the six directly invited teams in Seattle.
Third-place teams will advance to the Wild Card round in Seattle.
- Main Event - August 8th - August 13th, 2016
Sixteen teams play in a double elimination format over six days.
Eight teams begin in the upper bracket, eight in the lower bracket.
